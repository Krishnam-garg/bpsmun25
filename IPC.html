The International Press Corps (IPC) serves as the bridge between policy and perception, ensuring that every debate and decision is documented with integrity. As diplomats navigate interdimensional crises, the IPC will report, analyse, and challenge narratives, shaping the discourse of diplomacy. In a world where media wields immense power, we ask, “will journalism unite realities or become a tool of manipulation?” with pens sharper than swords and cameras capturing history across dimensions, the IPC stands as the beacon of truth in this multiversal dialogue.
